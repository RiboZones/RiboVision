About RiboVision

RiboEvo Theory releases version 1.06 of RiboVision. RiboVision is a modern open-source ribosome-information viewer website. It is designed for easy visual analysis. It is powered by a backend of pre-computed data, such as domain and helix definitions, proteins contacts, base pairing, sequence conservation, etc. Additionally, you may visualize your own data. If you can get your data into our easy format, you can visualize it.

This is version 1.06. RiboVision will be updated frequently. Please provide us with feedback.

RiboVision is located at http://apollo.chemistry.gatech.edu/RiboVision/index.html.

Source code is licensed under the MIT license , and is available on GitHub.

Our YouTube Channel contains video documentation.

Citing RiboVision

RiboVision has been submited to a journal for peer-review, but we have not received the decision yet. A publication citation will be provided when available. For citation before then, please refer to the preferred style of the journal you are submitting to. For example, in ACS style, the citation would appear like this.

Center for Ribosomal Origins and Evolution. RiboVision: Ribosome Visualization Suite. http://apollo.chemistry.gatech.edu/RiboVision/index.html (accessed Jun 13, 2013).
