Known Issues:

Missing Data:

  • Alignment data is not available for the 5S at this time.
  • Feel free to request data or to provide us with data. We will do our best to get it up there.

Technical Issues:

  • Certain OS/Browser combinations have a Jmol always on top bug. See Browser Compatibility.
  • Certain browser versions cannot support our user-generated data feature. See Browser Compatibility.
  • The Layer/Selection Manager or other windows may move too quickly or disappear. However, for now you can bring it back by resizing your browser window, and optionally resizing it back.
  • If it gets glitchy, try refreshing the page or even restarting your browser. If reproducible, send us a bug report.

Incomplete features:

  • Touchscreen support has not been finished.
  • Save PDF does not save line color gradients. They will come out as a solid color, based on the first residue in the pair.
  • The downloaded PDFs will display correctly, but they are not yet 100% illustrator compatible.

Unknown Issues:

Please report any unknown issues in as much detail as possible. Browser, version, OS, version, steps to reproduce the problem, etc.

Please also report feature requests, features changes, design decisions, etc. We may already be planning something you want, but we also might not have even thought about it yet.
